ComStock and ResStock are building stock-level diagnostic tools. States, municipalities, utilities and manufacturers can use them to identify high-impact improvements and make better program decisions.

With ComStock and ResStock, you can easily:

  • Discover how buildings can contribute to emissions targets.
  • Identify cost-effective upgrades for buildings in your area.
  • See the potential savings from individual segments, like buildings of a certain age or with certain heating fuels.
  • View state-specific fact sheets with potential savings and top upgrade measures.
  • Analyze custom scenarios — evaluate the potential of specific technologies, define your own cost-effectiveness evaluations, or get a high-granularity picture of your area's potential.
  • Visualize data and results.

Get started by signing up for a free ResStock or ComStock account.
