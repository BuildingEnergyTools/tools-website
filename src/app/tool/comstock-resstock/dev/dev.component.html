Links for Developers

ResStock

Use the free and open-source software yourself (or partner with the National Renewable Energy Laboratory or a third-party consultant). Analyze the scenarios of interest to you, whether you wish to evaluate the potential of a specific technology, define your own cost-effectiveness equations, or plug in hyperlocal data to get a high-granularity picture of the potential in a city or utility service area. Analysis results can be privately uploaded to the ResStock website for visualization.

 ComStock

Gain insights on the methodology and review the code and resource files used to generate the national scale analysis from the public ComStock GitHub repository.
