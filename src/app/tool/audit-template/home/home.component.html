Frustrated with the complexities of collecting and managing audit data? Audit Template makes it simple — think of it as an "easy button" for audits.

"The aspect I appreciate most about the Audit Template tool is the structure it has brought to our overall auditing process. It also saved time by avoiding mistakes in the field."

— Vic Batson, Boone Energy Co-Founder & CEO

With Audit Template, you can easily:

  • Implement an audit, retrocommissioning or building performance standard policy.
  • Manage private or municipal building stock data.
  • Submit audit reports online or through the mobile app.
  • Use the City Dashboard feature to track submissions and compliance.
  • Automatically confirm data quality and completeness.
  • Get exactly the data you need with a variety of ASHRAE Standard and custom reporting templates.
  • Generate reports, including energy consumption by fuel type and end use as well as upgrade costs and payback periods.
  • Export data to the SEED Platform database or other audit tools.
  • Share data with other people and systems in CSV, PDF, or BuildingSync XML formats.
  • Leverage expert support from DOE and the Pacific Northwest National Laboratory.

Get started by signing up for a free Audit Template account.
