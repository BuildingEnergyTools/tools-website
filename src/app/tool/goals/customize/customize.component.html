Customize

Because many of the building energy data tools are compatible with the BuildingSync file format and include application programming interfaces (APIs), you can integrate other applications from DOE or third parties into your BPS workflow. Whether you want to incorporate a customer relationship management (CRM) platform, proprietary vendor applications, or open-source resources like the Open Efficiency Platform (OEP) tools, the possibilities are limited only by your imagination. Visit the For Developers page for more information on workflow customization.
