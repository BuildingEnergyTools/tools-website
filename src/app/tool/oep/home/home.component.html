The OpenEfficiency Platform (OEP) is an easy way to share SEED Platform data with Salesforce. Use it to automate reporting, engage building owners and update public dashboards in real time.

It features a Salesforce managed package, MuleSoft API, implementation case studies, Salesforce email templates and more. Unlike custom solutions, the OEP can be quickly deployed without extensive training or prior experience.

With the OpenEfficiency Platform, you can easily:

  • Simplify and automate processes for building owners and program managers.
  • Streamline data collection, storage, verification and reporting.
  • Implement a simple, cost-effective and scalable reporting solution.
  • Improve operations while reducing program costs.
  • Reduce data processing time and errors.
  • Identify upgrade candidates and notify building owners.
  • Automate record updates from SEED to Salesforce.
  • Notify building owners of report and compliance status.
  • Get up and running quickly.

Discover how San Francisco's Department of Environment upgraded a spreadsheet-based audit and benchmarking program with the OEP in this case study.
