The DOE-funded OpenEfficiency Platform (OEP) lets efficiency programs connect Portfolio Manager and SEED to Salesforce, automating many reporting processes. It features a Salesforce managed package, a MuleSoft API, implementation case studies, Salesforce email templates and more. Unlike custom solutions, the OEP can be quickly adopted without extensive training or prior experience.

With the OpenEfficiency Platform, you can easily:

  • Simplify and automate processes for building owners and program managers.
  • Streamline data collection, storage, verification and reporting.
  • Implement a simple, cost-effective and scalable reporting solution.
  • Improve operations while reducing program costs.
  • Reduce data processing time and errors.
  • Identify upgrade candidates and notify building owners.
  • Automate record updates from SEED to Salesforce.
  • Use Salesforce to notify building owners of report and compliance status.
  • Incorporate Asset Score modeling to inform incentive programs and meet climate goals.
  • Do all the above with limited experience and training.

Discover how San Francisco's Department of Environment upgraded a spreadsheet-based audit and benchmarking program with the OEP in this case study.
