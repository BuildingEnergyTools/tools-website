The Building Efficiency Targeting Tool for Energy Retrofits (BETTER) reveals the energy performance of buildings and building portfolios. Use it to identify cost-saving improvements and target individual buildings and building systems for audits.

"The ability of the tool to go beyond benchmarking by offering energy efficiency measure recommendations is extremely valuable, particularly at times when on-site assessments are either impractical or not feasible."

— Eric Noller, Principal at Energy Resources Integration, LLC

With BETTER, you can easily:

  • See upgrade recommendations (technical and behavioral) with potential savings at the building and portfolio levels.
  • View potential energy, cost and GHG emission savings.
  • Identify immediate steps building owners can take to improve performance.
  • Compare a building's energy consumption to peers.
  • See the cause of performance differences (for example, an underperforming heating system).
  • Export data to the SEED Platform database or other audit tools.
  • Share data with other people and systems in CSV, PDF, or BuildingSync XML formats.
  • Incorporate BETTER’s analytical capabilities into your own software platforms and tools.

Best of all, BETTER only needs monthly energy consumption data to generate these insights.
