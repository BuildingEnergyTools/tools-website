The Building Efficiency Targeting Tool for Energy Retrofits (BETTER) identifies cost-saving energy and emissions reductions in buildings and portfolios, without site visits or complex modeling. Use it to identify immediate cost-saving operational and technological improvements and target buildings and systems for more in-depth audits and analysis.

With BETTER, you can easily:

  • Quantify actual operational energy, cost, and emissions reduction potential at the building and portfolio levels.
  • Identify efficiency measures (technical and behavioral) to immediately improve operational energy performance at the building and portfolio levels.
  • Compare and rank buildings across a portfolio according to annual electricity and fossil energy use intensity (EUI) and annual cost savings potential.
  • Target buildings with high cost-savings potential for audits or further analysis.
  • Identify buildings with high fossil EUI for electrification and decarbonization.
  • Assess the breakdown of annual utility costs and potential savings by load type (e.g., cooling, baseload, and heating) at the building level.
  • Investigate electricity and fossil fuel change-point models.
  • See how model coefficients benchmark against peers and which systems and equipment in a building may be underperforming.
  • Export data to the SEED Platform database or other audit tools.
  • Share data with other people and systems in CSV, PDF, or BuildingSync XML formats.
  • Incorporate BETTER's analytical capabilities into your own software platforms and tools.
  • Leverage expert support from DOE and Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.

Best of all, BETTER only needs monthly energy consumption data to generate these insights.
