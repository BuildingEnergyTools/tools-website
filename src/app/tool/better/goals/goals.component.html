Goals & Outcomes

Quantify savings potential, identify measures, and suggest areas for deeper evaluations.

BETTER provides analyses at the individual building and portfolio levels. BETTER’s Individual Building Report shows:

  • Annual building energy use and cost savings potential
  • Annual building GHG emissions reduction potential
  • Detailed energy efficiency recommendations for the building
  • Annual utility cost and savings breakdowns by end use (i.e., heating, cooling, baseload)
  • Monthly electric and fossil energy use trends (baseline and projections with assumed efficiency improvements)
  • Electricity and fossil fuel change-point models and model coefficient benchmarking

BETTER’s Portfolio Summary Report shows:

  • Annual portfolio energy use and cost savings potential
  • Annual portfolio GHG emissions reduction potential
  • Top five energy efficiency recommendations for the portfolio
  • Electric and fossil energy use intensity (EUI) and cost savings comparisons by building
  • Interactive charts and tables to sort, rank, and prioritize buildings for tune-ups, audits, etc.
