Goals & Outcomes

Identify measures and suggest areas for deeper evaluations.

BETTER provides analyses at the individual building and portfolio levels. BETTER’s Individual Building Report shows:

  • Annual energy, cost, and GHG emission reduction potential for the building
  • The building’s cost and savings breakdown
  • The original and potential reduced energy use trends
  • The change-point model and coefficients benchmarking details
  • The energy efficiency recommendation details for the building

BETTER’s Portfolio Summary Report shows:

  • Annual energy, cost, and GHG emission reduction potential for the portfolio
  • Top five energy efficiency improvement measures for the portfolio
  • An interactive energy use intensity (EUI) and cost savings potential comparison chart
  • A sortable table with energy use and savings potentials for each building, to allow ranking and investment prioritization across the portfolio.
