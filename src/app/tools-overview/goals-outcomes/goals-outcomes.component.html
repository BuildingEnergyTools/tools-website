Goals & Outcomes

Learn more about how the building energy data tools can help your organization realize its ultimate goals:

Analyze Building Stock

Analysis tools will help states, municipalities, utilities, and manufacturers identify the most impactful building stock improvements.

Target Efficiency Upgrade Areas

Identify measures and suggest areas for deeper evaluations.

Achieve Compliance

Use Asset Score to identify cost-effective energy efficiency measures to help buildings deliver EUI and GHGe metrics for compliance with a building performance policy.

Customize

Various tools and APIs are available to customize your workflow, connect to CRMs, add create data visualizations. Visit the Developers page to learn more about these tools and capabilities.
