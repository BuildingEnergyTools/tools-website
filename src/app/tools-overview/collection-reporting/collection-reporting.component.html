Data Collection & Reporting

Collecting building audit and energy consumption data is the first step in a building performance policy. Explore the tools on the right to learn more how they work together to make data collection & reporting easy, and how you can deploy them in your workflows.

ENERGY STAR Portfolio Manager is used by building owners to report their energy use. This information reveals how efficiently a building is being operated. Learn more .

The Building Energy Audit Template is a free, web-based tool used to collect, store and report building energy audit data. Audit data reveals the potential performance of a building based on its physical assets and equipment. Learn more.
