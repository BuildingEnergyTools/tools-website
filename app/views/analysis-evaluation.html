Analysis & Evaluation

You’ve collected your data with Audit Template and Portfolio Manager. You’ve stored it in a SEED Platform database. What’s next? It’s time to convert information into actionable intelligence using our analysis & evaluation tools. Explore the tools on the right to learn more how they work together to draw insight from information.

  • The ComStock and ResStock analysis tools combine large public and private data sources, statistical sampling, detailed sub-hourly building simulations, and high-performance computing to achieve unprecedented granularity and accuracy in modeling the U.S. building stock. Use them to identify which improvements to your building stock offer the most savings potential. Learn more.

  • The Building Efficiency Targeting Tool for Energy Retrofits (BETTER) provides users with visibility into the energy performance of buildings and portfolios (without expensive audits and complex modeling) to help identify immediate, cost-saving energy efficiency measures and to focus further assessments and studies. Learn more.

  • The Building Energy Asset Score tool assesses the efficiency of a building’s physical systems and produces an Energy Asset Score Report which includes an overall efficiency score (1-10), prioritized upgrade recommendations, estimated energy consumption by end usage, and other insights.

    Think of Asset Score as a “miles-per-gallon” sticker for a building. Learn more.
